Our Mission

At ZNN, our mission is to provide Towson University Computer Science students and faculty with the latest news, resources, and forums for vibrant community interaction. Our platform aims to enhance academic and social engagement through cutting-edge technology and comprehensive coverage of campus events and developments.

Who We Are

Founded in 2024 by a dedicated team of Towson University students, ZNN has grown to become a cornerstone of the Computer Science department's daily operations. We are committed to delivering accurate, informative, and engaging content tailored specifically for the Towson community.

Our History

Here's a brief timeline of our major milestones:

  • 2024 - ZNN was founded by a group of Towson University Students.
  • 2025 - Launched our first major platform upgrade introducing live news updates.
  • 2026 - Reached 10,000 active users primarily from the Towson community.

Technology We Use

At ZNN, we leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver seamless and dynamic news experiences. Our stack includes:

  • Cloud Services: Utilizing AWS and Azure for scalable cloud infrastructure.
  • Programming Languages: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, for frontend development.
  • Data Analytics: Tools like Google Analytics and custom-built analytics for user engagement metrics.
